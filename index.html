Books and Teaching

  • In 2021 and 2022, Karen Levy and I taught a new course
    entitled Choices and Consequences in Computing (INFO 1260 / CS 1340).
  • The course is designed at an introductory level with no formal prerequisites, and covers a range of
    ethical, societal, and policy implications of computing and information.
